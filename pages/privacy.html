Privacy Policy


At Marinduque State University, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.


  • Data Collection: We collect personal information such as names, student IDs, and email addresses for educational and administrative purposes.
  • Usage of Data: Your information is used to enhance your educational experience, including access to courses, university communications, and academic records.
  • Data Security: We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
  • Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information by contacting the university's administration.
