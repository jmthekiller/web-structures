Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


If you have questions about our university, you may find the answers below. These FAQs cover a wide range of topics, from admissions to campus life.


  • How do I apply to Marinduque State University? You can apply online through our admissions portal or visit the admissions office on campus.
  • What are the tuition fees? Tuition fees vary by program. Please visit the fees section on our website or contact the bursar's office for detailed information.
  • How can I access my academic records? Academic records can be accessed through the student portal using your university credentials.
  • Who can I contact for technical support? For technical support, please reach out to our IT helpdesk via email or phone. Contact details are available on the website.
